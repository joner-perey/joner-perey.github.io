Realtime Chat Feature

A Web-based feature designed to streamline communication among team members. The app provides a centralized platform for team members to share information, discuss ideas, and collaborate on projects. The messaging app allows users to create groups based on projects or departments, ensuring that relevant information is shared with the appropriate team members. The app also offers real-time messaging, allowing team members to quickly communicate with each other and resolve issues efficiently. The app also provides several features to enhance team collaboration, such as the ability to share files, images and save messages in Saved Chats Tab. This feature can highly improve team communication and productivity while reducing communication barriers and misunderstandings. This feature can also be integrated in other company's software tools.

Features

  • Real-time Messaging
  • Real-time Notifications
  • Save important messages in Saved Chats Tab
  • Send image or files
  • Can create group chats
  • No distractions/ads
