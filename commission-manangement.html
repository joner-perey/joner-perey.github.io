Commission Management System

A web based system designed to help manage and track commissions paid to their sales representatives or agents. The system can automate the calculation and payment of commissions, as well as provide detailed reports on commission performance and payouts. This can help to streamline their commission processes, reduce errors and disputes. Commission Management Systems can be integrated with other company's software tools.

Features

  • Generate reports
  • Generate contract using data record
  • Dashboard with Data Charts
  • Manage Agents and Commissions
  • Email Notifications
