Sales Monitoring System

A Web Based System that helps businesses track and analyze their sales performance. The system can provide real-time updates on sales activity, such as the number of sales made, the revenue generated, and the products or services sold. It can also generate detailed reports and analytics that can help businesses identify trends, measure sales growth, and evaluate the effectiveness of their sales strategies. Sales Monitoring Systems can be customized to meet the specific needs of different businesses, and can be integrated with other company's software tools.

Features

  • Inline-table Edit
  • Import/Export Spreadsheets
  • Bulk add data
  • Copy & Paste data from spreadsheet into "To be Add Table"
  • Multiple filter searching (can be use simultaneously)
