HR and Payroll System

A Web Based software tool that can help businesses manage employee data, track time and attendance, process payroll, and handle other HR-related tasks such as benefits administration, performance management, and leave management. The system can also automate many manual processes, such as calculating taxes and deductions, which can save time and reduce errors. HR and Payroll Systems can be customized to meet the specific needs of different businesses, and can be integrated with other company's software tools.

Features

  • Attendance tracking
  • Employee Management
  • Leave Management
  • Payroll Management
